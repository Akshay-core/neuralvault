About the book
In "The Art of Learning," eight-time national chess champion
and world champion martial artist Josh Waitzkin reveals the
invaluable lessons he has gained from his diverse experiences
in two fiercely competitive realms. Drawing on his journey, he
articulates essential principles of learning and performance
that empower readers to pursue their own life goals with
greater effectiveness and insight. With a first printing of
50,000 copies, this book offers a profound exploration of

About the author
Joshua Waitzkin is an accomplished American chess player,
martial arts competitor, and author, renowned for his early
achievements in the world of chess. A child prodigy, he
clinched the U.S. Junior Chess Championship titles in both
1993 and 1994, making history as the only individual to win
the National Primary, Elementary, Junior High School, High
School, U.S. Cadet, and U.S. Junior Closed chess
championships. His extraordinary journey served as the
inspiration for the acclaimed film *Searching for Bobby
Fischer*, which chronicles his formative years in the world of
chess.
